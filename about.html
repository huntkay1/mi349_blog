About Me!

Hi! I'm Kayla! I'm a junior majoring in Information Science at Michigan State University. A study a lot of tech-related topics in my studies, but in my free time, I love to cook. Trying out new dishes is my favorite, and so far, some of my favorites are:

  • chow mien
  • baklava
  • pasta arrabbiata
  • spring rolls with peanut dipping sauce

I've learned so many techniques throughout the years and it's so rewarding to be able to continusously try out more daunting recipes, using the skills that I have learned. Practice makes perfect and it really is rewarding to sit down and share a delicious meal with the people that I love. I hope you enjoy these recipes as much as I do!
